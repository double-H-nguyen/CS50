Video Games

Titles that have been occupying my time

Mass Effect Trilogy

The decisions you make will impact your story through the next game

An action role-playing video game that combines traditional western RPG mechanics and sci-fi story telling similar to Star Trek. Mass Effect is set within the Milky Way galaxy in the year 2183 where interstellar travel is possible through the use of mass transit devices called Mass Relays, a technology believed to have been built by an extinct alien race known as the Protheans. The term "Mass Effect" is defined as a form of mass-negating technology, allowing the creation of physics phenomena, like artificial gravity or FTL travel.

Warframe

Be a space ninja, and go for a power trip

Warframe is a free-to-play third-person action multiplayer game where you complete missions for parts, craft new weapons and gear, and repeat. The game doubles-down on engaging with gamers who thrives on progression and customization. In Warframe, players control members of the Tenno, a race of ancient warriors who have awoken from centuries of suspended animation far into Earth's future to find themselves at war in the planetary system with different factions. The Tenno use their powered Warframes along with a variety of weapons and abilities to complete missions. While many of the game's missions use procedurally-generated levels, newer updates have included large open world areas similar to other massively multiplayer online games as well as some story-specific missions with fixed level design. The game includes elements of shooting and melee games, parkour, and role-playing to allow players to advance their Tenno with improved gear.

Satisfactory

Build and optimize your factories for the sake of engineering

Satisfactory is a factory simulation game where you have creative freedom to decide how to build your automated machines. The player, a pioneer, is dropped onto an alien planet with a handful of tools and must harvest the planet's natural resources to construct increasingly complex factories for automating all resource needs. The goal is to produce a variety of resources to unlock the next tier, which will expand the scope of how you produce future resources. As factories become more complicated, problems will need to be solved and designs refactored, which will appeal to the engineering and programming minds.
